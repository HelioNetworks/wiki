# Tommy

Tommy is one of our fastest and most stable servers, with its hardware being amongst the newest between our servers. Tommy has a high uptime of 100% and it cannot drop to anything lower than 99% uptime. Because of its stability and speed, it is recommended to anyone that needs a fast an stable production server.
